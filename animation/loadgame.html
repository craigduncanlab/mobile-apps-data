Where can you find CSV data files to load here?

This animator will recognise the Junior Cricket Scorer CSV files that you can email from your device.

You can support the development of this cricket match animator by purchasing a copy of my Junior Cricket Scorer for iOS from the App Store.

To obtain the CSV files, score your matches using Junior Cricket Scorer on iPhone or iPad, then email the results to yourself, and save the CSV files to your PC.


CSV formats other than Junior Cricket Scorer

The comprehensive 'CricSheet' data produced by Stephen Rushe(*) for several cricket formats, including BBL and Test matches, can also be loaded here.

Instructions:

1. Go to https://cricsheet.org/downloads/#experimental and download one of the new' CSV format zip files (e.g. https://cricsheet.org/downloads/bbl_male_csv2.zip).

2. Extract the zip files to your computer so there are folders with CSV files in them.

3. Load up an individual CSV file from your PC into this page.

(*) See Twitter @srushe and @cricsheet
